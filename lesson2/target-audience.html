Target Audience

The Local (Persona 1)

"The Local" refers to anyone in the local community or area that is to be served by this website. The website will provide up to date weather info as well as local events that are going on. These local event reminders are provided as a planning tool in relation to upcoming weather. This user will tend to use the site frequently and will be more likely to do so from their mobile phone, but on ocasion will be using their PC to access the site.

The Visitor (Persona 2)

"The Visitor" while similar to the local is mainly there to mainly see major events. These events will be bolded/highlighted to show major importance for visitors. For example special events relating to carnivals or festivals will be under this distinction. This user will mainly be accessing the site from the desktop, they plan in advanced so knowing what is happening when they go to the area. The site will provide them with the ability to look at important major events.

Scenarios

  1. Family is looking for current weather information to plan their outing for a weekend in the local park. (The Local)
  2. City is looking for weather forcast for planning a parade and whether or not they need to set up awnings to protect electrical equipment. (The Local)
  3. Family is traveling through the city on an upcoming trip, they are looking for things to do and what the weather is like when they are closer to when they go. (The Visitor)
  4. A family member is visiting from out of town and the family wants to plan activities to do together. (The Local / The Visitor)
  5. A family is looking for things to do over the summer. They are looking for both major and minor events (The Local)
