Target Audience

Locals Headed To The River:

Locals who would like to check the weather for outdoor activities.

Vacationers:

People visiting the area that would like to enjoy nature.

Scenarios:

Fishing- Checking if the weather is too good and the river will be crowded.

Traveling- Seeing if the weather will be pleasant enough for outdoor activities.

Rafting- Checking to see if it is safe to go rafting.

Swimming- Checking if it is warm enough to go swimming.

Hiking- Checking if it is rainin on the trail.
