Target Audience

The Local (Persona 1)

"The Local" refers to those in the local community that will be able to utilize this website. The website will provide up to date weather info as well as local events. These local event reminders are provided as a planning tool in regard to weather. This user will use the site frequently and will often visit on their phone, with some site visits via PC.

The Visitor (Persona 2)

"The Visitor" Will visit the cities and our site to see major events. These events will be bold and highlighted to mark significance for visitors. For example special events relating to concerts or festivals will be marked with this distinction. This user will be accessing the site from their desktop to plan visits in advance. The site will provide them with the ability to look at important major events and weather during their stay.

Scenarios

  1. Family is looking for current weather information to plan their outing for a weekend visiting the park. (The Local)
  2. City is looking for weather forcast to aid in planning a parade. (The Local)
  3. Family is traveling through the city on an upcoming trip, they are looking for things to do and what the weather will be like. (The Visitor)
  4. Family member is visiting from out of town and the family wants to plan activities to do together. (The Local / The Visitor)
  5. Family is planning for the summer. They are looking into which local events seem worth attending. (The Local)
