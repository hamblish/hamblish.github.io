Typography

Headings

Font Choice: Roboto Slab

Reason: Roboto Slab works well with the Roboto font (the body font in this case) for headings. It provides a compact but readable heading stlye while looking clean, but sharp, to grab the attention of the reader when necessary (as it is a serif font).

Body

Font Choice: Roboto

Reason: Roboto is one of my favorite fonts. Having been used by Google for both their websites and the Android operating system, it is a very easy on the eyes font. It provides a highly readable font that is best used when you have large portions of text. With Google using it as their default font it also provides a seemless look to it when comparing it to the fonts the users previously saw when navigating to the site.
