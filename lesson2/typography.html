Typography

Headings

Font Choice: Roboto Slab

Reason: Roboto Slab works well for headings with the Roboto font for body text. It provides a compact and readable stlye. The font is serif and sharp, to grab the attention of the reader where intended.

Body

Font Choice: Roboto

Reason: Used by Google and Android systems, the font is easy on the eyes. It provides a highly readable format, best used for large portions of text. The font provides a seemless look when comparing it to others.
