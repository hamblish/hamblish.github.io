About Orcus

Orcus grew up in a broken home his father never really supported him and at a young age he knew he wanted to be a part of something bigger. He set out to create something that would bring people together. He always loved the river near his home growing up and he thought of the symbolism of different paths and branches that the river would take it would bring it all back to the same place. He decided then and there that he wanted to be a part of it and started up his rafting retreat. When you come rollin on the river you don't have to worry. The river brings you back home.
